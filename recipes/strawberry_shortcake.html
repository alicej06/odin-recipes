Strawberry Shortcake

Home

Description

Delicious strawberry shortcake!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the milk and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently a few times. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick rectangle and cut out rounds using a biscuit cutter.
  5. Place the rounds on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from the oven and let cool on a wire rack.
  6. While the shortcakes are baking, prepare the strawberries. In a bowl, combine the sliced strawberries and 2 tablespoons of sugar. Let them macerate for at least 30 minutes.
  7. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  8. To assemble the strawberry shortcakes, slice the cooled shortcakes in half horizontally. Spoon some of the macerated strawberries onto the bottom half, followed by a generous dollop of whipped cream. Place the top half of the shortcake on top and add more strawberries and whipped cream if desired.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade strawberry shortcake!
