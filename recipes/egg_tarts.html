Egg Tarts

Description

Delicious egg tarts!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and powdered sugar. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add the egg yolk and cold water, mixing until the dough comes together. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface and cut into circles to fit your tart pans. Press the dough into the pans and trim any excess.
  5. In a saucepan, heat the milk and heavy cream until just simmering. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar and eggs until well combined. Gradually pour the hot milk mixture into the egg mixture, whisking constantly. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the custard filling into the prepared tart shells.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the custard is set and the crust is golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool before serving. Enjoy your homemade egg tarts!
